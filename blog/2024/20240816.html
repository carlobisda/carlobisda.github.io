My Federer Racket Collection

I am such a Wilson nerd, all because of my GOAT Roger Federer. He really was
the one that really fueled the fire of love for tennis. I try to take and learn
as much as I can from him.

And with that, of course I am trying to collect all of the racket models he ever
used. Granted I know that most pros' say they endorse a racket but are completely
using a different type of racket but for the most part, I think Federer somewhat
used the model similar to the retail version, with slight difference, and probably
high end materials in the mold.

Like I believe for sure he used the Ncode and the Kfactor as they were so different
and was clearly not like previouse models. Maybe in the RF97 phase, he was using
something different but I think the retail models are close to what he was using.
