The Concquerer

The process is kinda fun it its' own way but TBH, I prefer winning and then moving
onto the next goal to concquer I know the modern "meta" for lifehacking or productivity
hacks is that of enjoying the process, don't be so tied up to the goal, etc.

That might work for most people but not for me. And I'm not even being disparaging about
this, this is purely my opinion and based on my personality and attitude about life.

Eastern philosophy often emphasizes the journey over the destination, suggesting that the
process of striving to achieve the goal is where one finds true meaning and value. I whole
heartedly disagree with this. I don't know how to explain it but personally, it feels like I'm
running away from my goals. I mean it sounds nice to trust the process (KEKW) and that detaching
oneself from the goal will lessen the mental and physical burder and I agress with this somewhat
but I don't completely agree on detaching oneself from it.

Personally, I like to be reminded of my goal; it helps my drive, it inspires me to go harder,
be smarter on how I go about reaching my goal. Maybe it's the next generation type of thinking
but most people are gamers and what do gamers like seeings? Their achievement badges going up lol
It is so satisfying when your complete a task (in game) and seeing that achievement badge up in
dashboard. Or farming for hours just so you can get a certain gear set, etc etc.

If all else fails, I just stick to my personal philosophy of balance, have one eye on the goal while
one eye on the process, just personally balance is the way. Objectively, I feel like 60% on the goal
and 40% on the process is my actual paradigm.
