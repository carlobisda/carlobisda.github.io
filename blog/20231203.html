Life Build

for the game and it made me think about my life - what is the life build that I
want.

So much things have happened recently that I feel like I've kinda been outta touch
with myself, my goal and path has kinda been blurry - like I know what I need to do
but the determination is not there.. I need to get excited more.

This has been the kind of thinking I've been having for the past couple of months, I
know what I need to do but my energy is just in an all time low. It needs to stop,
I need to keep moving forward.

Or maybe I'm just fatigued? I've been at it for so many years now that maybe I'm just
burnt out from the grind.

So how do I get out of this?

With that said, with what I'm feeling currently - it doesn't hurt to have a plan. Lets
take baby steps till I feel inspired to get a move on. TBH, as I'm writing this - I am
getting a little bit energized. Let me align everything in my life according to my plan.

so it is my will, so shall it be done..
