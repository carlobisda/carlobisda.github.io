Playnite

Such a cool program/app.

I was looking for something that I can organize what games I owned
and I was thinking of writing my own but I ended up looking at cloud
option but they kinda sucked, as in I had to input everything manually
- no good option to upload a batch file, till I accidentally found Playnite

I love that I can sync my online accounts to it, and I just need to manually
add my physical games. It also syncs to an online game database which has nifty
thumbnails and info added which is a nice QOL touch. Also has a counter that shows
how much you've played the game, etc etc

I'm sure there are other features that I've missed but this is just too cool nocap.
