Vibe Coding is Hella Fun

One of the good things that came out of the advancement of AI is vibe coding.
Its so much easier for me to code now, well first off I'm just an amatuer but
I know enough to debug or figure out an issue.

So now, it's so much easier to prototype apps and programs and just customize
as I see fit. Totally up my strikezone.

I always tended to lean to RAD and using programs that can help me churn out as
fast as I humanly can, Prototype and release, that's the name of my game. I just
functional in that sense, I'm the type of person that will release imperfection
rather than grind myself to reach some sort of "perfect" illusion. Don't get me
wrong, there's a certain level of standard I follow before I release anything but
I'm not a perfectionist that needs every line of code allocated and counted.

live fast, die young, and leave a good looking corpse
