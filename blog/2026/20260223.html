Not A Content Creator

I am not a content creator, I hate that term ngl. Personally, I'm just a
an artist I guess - so sharing is kinda a necessary thing and while I do
think there's really not a difference, I really do think they cheapened the
whole idea.

Basically, they've just gone far too end of the spectrum of being a corpo
dog - anything for the sake of the almight $$$. I mean everyone wants and
needs money but I guess it's just a stylistic disagreement, like they gone
too in front of it, like there's no shame.

But my point is, I'm not a content creator - I like to share my journey but
but I'm not here to make money out of my life. It would be nice to make some
money off my journey but making money is not the focus of me sharing my life.

I just thought I'd brain dump this here since I always seem to feel like I
want to be a content creator, always making instagram and youtube profiles,
always consuming creator drama, always pining the "why them not me" type of
thinking.

Brother, please, get this out of your head. You're not some extroverted mega
-star. You're the type who is calm, cool and collected. You don't chase clout
, it comes to you - quality over quantity. You want to deal with real movers,
not the masses. It kinda sounds douchey but that's how you work.

You like the shadows, you thrive in there. Don't waste your time pretending
to be someone else.

live fast, die young, and leave a good looking corpse
