Retiring RSL (FORREAL)

I wrote before that I was going to retire from playing Raid Shadow
Legends but I changed my mind since I found a way to kind circumvent
my initial problem of taking time away from me.

I've been thinking about it again recently and I'm not just a gamer, I
admit - I don't find pleasure in relaxing playing video games. I am
just wired to get better at them - I don't find playing it casually fun
, even when I was playing Super Mario, I was trying to find the META
way to beat it.

So I don't find value in keep playing RSL, its taking up my time while
not contributing any skill and value. It was fun, don't get me wrong,
in a dumb game kinda way but if I were to respect my time, then I should
move on from this.

Goodbye Raid Shadow Legends, it was fun.
