My Everday Carry [2023]

The concrete ninja in me has always been keen to ideas like urban prepping,
camping, etc - such that I've always had some sort of EDC kit on me whenever I
leave the house. This is seperate from my bugout bag which is more customized
for a more post-*.* survival scenario where I feel like I would end up in the
rural/forest area.

So my normal everyday carry consist of.....
Cellphone: Kinda really important in modern times, so useful.
Lighter: Because I'm a smoker (lol) but also important if you need fire.
Multi-tool: Never really found a use for it till I got a car and then just
little things that I need it for start popping up, especially for prying things.
Pills: Meds for headache and allergy.
Self Defense Tool: I have one but mostly for intimidations, my brain and my
physical strength is my main self defense tools.

Some might ask, where's my emergency med kit (lol). This EDC is mainly when I go out
with some tools to help me out, not necessarily for me to survive when the zombie
apocalypse occurs. Like I said, my bugout bad exists for that reason (lol). And to be fair,
it's situational as when I drive, I don't really take some stuff or when I'm out skateboarding
obviously the things I carry differentiate.

It's just the act and practice of having an EDC with you gives off a sense of vigilance
that I think is helpful and productive for a person.
