Paradigm

This is a repository, but serves more as a manifesto and a personal
tome of wisdom, which also serves as a reminder for myself (because to
be honest, my mind goes into alot of paths).

Me?

Fundamentally, I have conservative beliefs in regards to humans but will
not fight progress. Most of the time, I believe in the individual potential
for greatness while disdain the human herd mentality.

With that said, I have very human tendencies; I enjoy gambling, games,
physical activity, technological lust, music, etc.

I don't have any real formal education or credentials past high school. For
the most part, it's been through life experiences and self study.

Humanist

I've been a humanist at early age and I will probably start to delve into it
more in some way, shape and form through online means.

My ideas and philosophies are currently in a state of work in progress. There
is so much to learn and synthesize and once I think I have some sort of grasp
or footing on a matter, a new term or idea pops out that I have to investigate
and learn.

Current Events

Most people won't understand, nor would care (which is understandable) but we
are living in a time of changes. I mean as living beings, we are always changing
but if you haven't noticed - we have been shifting in quite a rapid pace and I
think as a race - we weren't prepared on how technology would affect us.

Even I was affected by this and I had to step back and rebalance my life priorities
to accomodate this wave of change.

GameStop Squeeze

I saw no market manipulation on the retail traders - just a prime example of the human
condition. I mean they saw a way to stick it to the man after many years of being
kicked down and the same entities being helped out after their mistakes while the muggles
were left to hold the bag.

I'm more suspicious about the financial institutions who did all they can to stop the squeeze
, Robinhood being one of the main bad actors. What kind of proof do I have? Just historical facts
which makes me believe that is their modus operandi.

BLM

I don't really have a take on this since this is a pretty human action if I really thought about.

Personally, I feel that all human lives matter but I can see why the need to take such a
