Paradigm

This is a repository, but serves more as a manifesto and a personal
tome of wisdom, which also serves as a reminder for myself (because to
be honest, my mind goes into alot of paths).

Humanist

I've been a humanist at early age and I will probably start to delve into it
more in some way, shape and form through online means.

My ideas and philosophies are currently in a state of work in progress. There
is so much to learn and synthesize and once I think I have some sort of grasp
or footing on a matter, a new term or idea pops out that I have to investigate
and learn.

Hacking

Since I got my old PC, there was no way to escape the path of being a hacker. For
a more intimate reading that reflects my personal ideas of hacking; google
"The Hacker Manifesto" and "The Hacker Crackdown" by Bruce Sterling

I'm essentially a cyberpunk kiddie LOL.

Relationships

I've on the mind of a charitable eye for an eye, tooth for a tooth, it's all situational
for me I suppose. Fundamentally, I truly do believe for an eye for an eye scenario; like
if you try to fook me, then you just gave me the pass.

Does that mean I have to? That's left to my discretion. My thought process regarding on
taking action is how grave the offence is? Is it time consuming? And how my pleasure would I
incur from it. Sounds psychotic, yeppers.

But to be fair, I do understand that humans makes mistakes so it really just boils down to
how fooked up said offense was or my mood lol.

Current Events

Most people won't understand, nor would care (which is understandable) but we
are living in a time of changes. I mean as living beings, we are always changing
but if you haven't noticed - we have been shifting in quite a rapid pace and I
think as a race - we weren't prepared on how technology would affect us.

Even I was affected by this and I had to step back and rebalance my life priorities
to accomodate this wave of change.

Politics

For all intents and purposes, I am moderate for most of the ideals but I tend to lean towards ideals
that are a tad extreme and centers around human development. (Hyper-Centrist, is that a word LOL?)

Philosophy

My personal philosophy is a mix of Eastern and Western thinkings, I am still working on
crystalizing my own philosophy ideals into some sort of concrete piece of work but for now,
I will just list the notable thinkers (or ideas) that has influenced my way of thinking.

Bruce Lee, Miyamoto Musashi, Socrates, Carl Jung, Friedrich Nietzsche, Jordan Peterson (WIP),
Che Guevara, The Clash, Jack White, Stoicism, Bushido, Taoism, Dadaism, Minimalism,
Essentialism.
