Universe 2077

Universe 2077 is my multi-media science fiction/cyberpunk project part
of a bigger multi-genre universe. I plan to create and release various media
projects such as short stories, video games, etc, in this universe and hope to
have a very "alive" piece of art in the end.

My work here is my love letter to the cyberpunk genre as I've been greatly
influenced by it; Neuromancer, Snow Crash, Cowboy Bebop, Akira, Minority Report and
Psycho Pass (to name a few) has been my influences in regards to content I mainly
enjoy.

“Things aren’t different. Things are things.”
