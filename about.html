About Me

Introverted most of the time, my personal philosophy in dealing with life in general is observe
quietly but take action swiftly. I maintain a healthy balance between risk and caution.
The notable thinkers that has influenced my way of thinking include Bruce Lee, Miyamoto Musashi,
Socrates, Carl Jung, Friedrich Nietzsche, Jordan Peterson (WIP), Che Guevara, The Clash, Jack White,
Stoicism, Bushido, Taoism, Dadaism and Minimalism.

My top 5 movies are (in no particular order); The Big Sleep, The New Guy, The Patriot, Into the Wild,
and 300.

My top 5 bands are ACDC, Jimi Hendrix, Nirvana, The Clash and Rory Gallagher. (p.s: I have a soft spot
for T Swift KEKW)

To be honest, I believe the scope of my "self" can't be captured within a few lines of code and that I
can only provide snapshots of it currently. For the most part, my fundamental core values of balance,
progression and strength will not change - the resulting ideas and philosophies that spawn because of
how life has dealt my cards and the outcomes that shaped me are always changing as I weave through the
world while learning new things about it, and myself.

I wish my fellow travellers well.
